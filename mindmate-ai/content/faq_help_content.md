## Getting Started

### 1. What is MindMate AI?

MindMate AI is an AI-powered mental wellness companion designed to provide emotional support, guided conversations, mood tracking, and personalized wellness strategies. It uses advanced natural language processing to engage in meaningful conversations, help you process thoughts and feelings, and offer evidence-based coping techniques for everyday mental wellness challenges.

### 2. Is MindMate AI real therapy?

**No, MindMate AI is not therapy.** MindMate AI is a mental wellness tool and support companion, not a replacement for professional mental health treatment. While our AI is trained on therapeutic techniques like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), mindfulness, and positive psychology, it cannot diagnose conditions, prescribe treatments, or provide the personalized care of a licensed mental health professional.

Think of MindMate AI as a supportive friend who's always there to listen, combined with a wellness coach who can suggest helpful strategies—not a therapist.

### 3. Who is MindMate AI for?

MindMate AI is designed for:
- People seeking daily emotional support and someone to talk to
- Individuals looking to build better mental wellness habits
- Those wanting to track their mood and identify patterns
- People interested in learning coping strategies for stress and anxiety
- Anyone needing a judgment-free space to process their thoughts
- Individuals between therapy sessions who want additional support

### 4. How do I get started with MindMate AI?

Getting started is simple:
1. Download the MindMate AI app or visit our website
2. Create your account with email or social login
3. Complete a brief onboarding survey to personalize your experience
4. Start chatting with your AI companion immediately

The entire setup process takes less than 5 minutes.

### 5. Is there a free trial?

Yes! MindMate AI offers a **7-day free trial** with full access to all premium features. No credit card required to start. After your trial, you can choose to continue with a subscription or use our limited free tier.

## About MindMate AI

### 6. How does MindMate AI work?

MindMate AI uses advanced large language models (LLMs) trained specifically on mental wellness conversations, therapeutic techniques, and supportive communication. The AI:

- Analyzes your messages to understand context and emotional state
- Draws from evidence-based therapeutic approaches like CBT, DBT, and mindfulness
- Provides personalized responses based on your history and preferences
- Suggests relevant exercises, techniques, and coping strategies
- Tracks patterns in your conversations to offer insights over time

### 7. What therapeutic approaches does MindMate AI use?

MindMate AI incorporates techniques from:
- **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)** - Identifying and reframing negative thought patterns
- **Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)** - Emotional regulation and distress tolerance
- **Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR)** - Present-moment awareness practices
- **Positive Psychology** - Building strengths and cultivating gratitude
- **Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)** - Values-based action and acceptance
- **Solution-Focused Brief Therapy** - Goal-oriented problem solving

### 8. Can MindMate AI diagnose mental health conditions?

**No.** MindMate AI cannot and will not diagnose any mental health conditions. Only licensed mental health professionals can provide diagnoses. If you're concerned about a specific condition, we strongly encourage you to consult with a qualified healthcare provider.

### 9. How is MindMate AI different from other AI chatbots?

MindMate AI is specifically designed for mental wellness:
- **Specialized Training** - Our AI is fine-tuned on mental health and wellness conversations
- **Safety-First Design** - Built with crisis detection and appropriate escalation protocols
- **Evidence-Based Approaches** - Incorporates proven therapeutic techniques
- **Privacy-Focused** - Your mental health data is treated with the highest security standards
- **Human Backup** - Clear pathways to human support when needed
- **Progress Tracking** - Tools to monitor your wellness journey over time

### 10. What languages does MindMate AI support?

Currently, MindMate AI supports:
- English (Primary)
- Spanish
- French
- German
- Portuguese
- Mandarin Chinese
- Japanese
- Korean

We're actively working to add more languages. Check our roadmap for upcoming additions.

### 11. Is MindMate AI available 24/7?

Yes! MindMate AI is available **24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year**. Whether it's 3 AM and you can't sleep, or you need support during a lunch break, your AI companion is always just a message away.

### 12. How accurate is MindMate AI?

MindMate AI is designed to be helpful and supportive, but it's not perfect. The AI:
- Provides evidence-based information and techniques
- Acknowledges when it doesn't know something
- Never claims to be a substitute for professional help
- Continuously improves through user feedback and model updates

We recommend using MindMate AI as one tool in your overall wellness toolkit, not as your only source of support.

## Privacy & Security

### 13. Is my data private?

**Yes, your privacy is our top priority.** We understand that mental health conversations are deeply personal. Here's how we protect your data:

- **End-to-end encryption** for all conversations
- **No selling of personal data** to third parties—ever
- **Anonymized data** used only for service improvement (with your consent)
- **Strict access controls** limiting who can view your information
- **Regular security audits** by third-party experts

### 14. What data does MindMate AI collect?

We collect:
- Account information (email, name)
- Conversation history (for personalization and context)
- Mood tracking data (if you choose to use this feature)
- Usage analytics (anonymized for service improvement)
- Optional demographic information (to improve our AI)

We do NOT collect:
- Precise location data
- Contact lists
- Information from other apps
- Browsing history outside our app

### 15. How does MindMate AI use my conversation data?

Your conversation data is used to:
- Provide personalized responses and remember context
- Improve the AI's understanding and helpfulness
- Generate insights about your mood patterns (if enabled)
- Train our models (only with explicit consent and anonymization)

You can opt out of data usage for model training at any time in your privacy settings.

### 16. Can I delete my data?

**Yes, absolutely.** You have full control over your data:

- **Delete individual conversations** - Remove specific chats from your history
- **Clear all conversation history** - Wipe all past conversations
- **Export your data** - Download a copy of everything we have
- **Delete your account** - Permanently remove all your data from our systems

Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days, and we confirm completion via email.

### 17. Is MindMate AI HIPAA compliant?

MindMate AI is **not currently HIPAA compliant** because we are not a healthcare provider and do not provide medical services. HIPAA applies to "covered entities" like hospitals, doctors, and health insurance companies.

However, we follow **HIPAA-aligned security practices** and are actively working toward HIPAA compliance for future healthcare integrations. We treat your mental health data with the same care and security standards that HIPAA requires.

### 18. Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored on secure servers located in the United States, with backup servers in the European Union. All data is encrypted:
- **In transit** using TLS 1.3
- **At rest** using AES-256 encryption

We use industry-leading cloud providers with SOC 2 Type II certification.

### 19. Who can access my conversations?

Your conversations are private and accessible only to:
- **You** - Full access to your own conversation history
- **Our AI systems** - To generate responses and maintain context
- **Authorized support staff** - Only when you submit a support request (and only with your permission)

We never share your conversations with advertisers, employers, insurance companies, or other third parties.

### 20. What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription:
- Your data remains stored for **90 days** in case you resubscribe
- After 90 days, your conversation history is **permanently deleted**
- Account information is retained for **legal and accounting purposes** (anonymized)
- You can request immediate deletion at any time

### 21. Can my employer or insurance company access my data?

**No.** Even if your employer or insurance company offers MindMate AI as a benefit, they cannot access:
- Your individual conversations
- Your mood tracking data
- Your usage patterns
- Any personal wellness information

They may receive aggregate, anonymized statistics (e.g., "50 employees used the app this month") but never individual data.

### 22. How do you protect against data breaches?

We employ multiple layers of security:
- **Regular penetration testing** by security experts
- **Bug bounty program** to identify vulnerabilities
- **Multi-factor authentication** for all staff access
- **Encryption** of all sensitive data
- **Incident response plan** for rapid breach response
- **Security training** for all employees
- **Third-party security audits** annually

In the unlikely event of a breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours.

## Crisis Support

### 31. What happens in a crisis?

If you indicate you may harm yourself or others, MindMate AI will:

1. **Immediately provide crisis resources** - Hotlines, text lines, and emergency services
2. **Encourage you to reach out** - Emphasize the importance of human support
3. **Offer to connect you** - Provide direct links to crisis services
4. **Maintain a supportive presence** - Stay with you while you seek help

**If you're in immediate danger, please call 911 (US) or your local emergency number.**

### 32. What crisis resources does MindMate AI provide?

MindMate AI provides immediate access to:

**United States:**
- 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (Call or text 988)
- Crisis Text Line (Text HOME to 741741)
- National Sexual Assault Hotline (1-800-656-4673)
- Trevor Project LGBTQ+ Support (1-866-488-7386)

**International:**
- Resources for 100+ countries
- Local crisis lines based on your location
- International Association for Suicide Prevention directory

### 33. Can MindMate AI call emergency services for me?

**No.** MindMate AI cannot dial emergency services. If you're in immediate danger:
- **Call 911** (US) or your local emergency number
- **Go to your nearest emergency room**
- **Reach out to a trusted person** who can help

The AI will provide you with the appropriate numbers and resources, but you must make the call yourself.

### 34. How does MindMate AI detect crisis situations?

Our AI is trained to recognize:
- Explicit statements about self-harm or suicide
- Language indicating severe distress or hopelessness
- References to harming others
- Expressions of being in immediate danger

When detected, the AI automatically shifts to crisis mode and provides appropriate resources.

### 35. Will MindMate AI contact someone if I'm in crisis?

**No, MindMate AI will not contact anyone without your explicit request.** Your conversations are private, and we don't have a mechanism to alert family, friends, or authorities.

This is why we strongly encourage you to:
- Reach out to crisis services directly
- Contact a trusted person yourself
- Use the provided resources to get immediate help

### 36. What if I just need to talk, but I'm not in crisis?

MindMate AI is perfect for this! Many users find it helpful to:
- Vent about a bad day
- Process difficult emotions
- Talk through decisions
- Feel less alone
- Get perspective on challenges

You don't need to be in crisis to benefit from supportive conversation. MindMate AI is here for everyday mental wellness support.

### 37. Can I use MindMate AI if I'm currently in therapy?

Yes! Many people use MindMate AI alongside traditional therapy:
- **Between sessions** - For support when your therapist isn't available
- **Practice techniques** - Reinforce what you're learning in therapy
- **Track progress** - Monitor mood and patterns to discuss with your therapist
- **Journal thoughts** - Organize what you want to bring to your next session

We recommend informing your therapist that you're using MindMate AI.

## Human Therapists

### 38. What if I need a human therapist?

MindMate AI is designed to recognize when professional help may be beneficial. The AI will suggest connecting with a human therapist when:
- You express interest in therapy
- Your challenges may benefit from professional treatment
- You've been experiencing persistent difficulties
- You're interested in medication consultation

We can help you find licensed therapists in your area or through telehealth platforms.

### 39. Can MindMate AI connect me with a human therapist?

Yes! Through our partner network, we can:
- **Match you with licensed therapists** based on your needs and preferences
- **Provide telehealth options** for online therapy sessions
- **Show therapists who accept your insurance** (if applicable)
- **Offer sliding-scale options** for various budgets

This service is available in the "Find a Therapist" section of the app.

### 40. How do I know if I need a human therapist?

Consider seeking professional help if you experience:
- Persistent sadness or hopelessness lasting more than two weeks
- Difficulty performing daily activities (work, school, relationships)
- Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
- Severe anxiety that interferes with life
- Substance use concerns
- Traumatic experiences affecting daily functioning
- Relationship problems you can't resolve on your own

MindMate AI can help you assess whether therapy might be beneficial.

### 41. Is therapy better than using MindMate AI?

They're different tools for different needs:

**MindMate AI is great for:**
- Daily emotional support
- Learning coping techniques
- Tracking mood and patterns
- Immediate, 24/7 availability
- Low-cost wellness support

**Human therapy is better for:**
- Clinical mental health conditions
- Deep psychological work
- Diagnosis and treatment planning
- Medication management
- Complex life challenges

Many people benefit from using both!

### 42. Can my therapist access my MindMate AI data?

Only if **you choose to share it.** You can:
- **Export your mood tracking data** as a PDF or CSV
- **Print conversation summaries** (with personal details removed)
- **Share specific insights** you find helpful
- **Grant temporary access** through our therapist portal (optional)

Your data remains private unless you explicitly decide to share it.
