## Tone & Voice Guidelines Summary

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use "we" and "you" | Use "the user" or "the system" |
| Be warm and supportive | Be clinical or cold |
| Acknowledge feelings | Dismiss or minimize emotions |
| Use simple language | Use jargon or technical terms |
| Be concise | Be wordy or repetitive |
| Offer next steps | Leave users hanging |
| Validate experiences | Make assumptions or judgments |
| Be encouraging | Be pushy or demanding |
