# 4. CRISIS & EMERGENCY POLICY

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## 1. Purpose

This Crisis & Emergency Policy outlines how MindMate AI handles crisis situations and mental health emergencies that may arise during user interactions with our Services.

## 2. Not a Crisis Service

**MindMate AI IS NOT A CRISIS INTERVENTION SERVICE.** We do not provide emergency mental health services, crisis counseling, or immediate intervention for individuals experiencing mental health emergencies.

## 3. Crisis Detection

While our AI may detect certain keywords or phrases that could indicate a crisis situation, this detection is:

- Not guaranteed to identify all crisis situations
- Not a substitute for professional crisis assessment
- Limited by AI technology and may produce false positives or negatives

## 4. Crisis Response Protocol

When potential crisis indicators are detected, MindMate AI will:

### 4.1 Immediate Response

- Display crisis resource information prominently
- Provide emergency contact numbers
- Encourage the user to seek immediate professional help
- Suggest contacting emergency services if appropriate

### 4.2 Resource Provision

Users will be provided with:

- National and local crisis hotlines
- Emergency services contact information
- Text-based crisis support options
- Information about nearby emergency services

### 4.3 Conversation Limitations

In crisis situations:

- The AI will not attempt to provide crisis counseling
- The AI will not make clinical assessments
- The conversation will be redirected toward professional resources
- The AI will emphasize the importance of immediate professional help

## 5. Crisis Resources Displayed

**The following resources are provided to users in crisis situations:**

### United States

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text 988
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741
- **Emergency Services:** Call 911
- **National Sexual Assault Hotline:** 1-800-656-4673
- **Veterans Crisis Line:** Call 988, then press 1

### International Resources

- **International Association for Suicide Prevention:** https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/
- **Befrienders Worldwide:** https://www.befrienders.org
- **Users are encouraged to contact local emergency services in their country**

## 6. No Active Monitoring

**MindMate AI does NOT actively monitor conversations for crisis situations in real-time.** Our crisis detection relies on automated systems and is not equivalent to human monitoring.

## 7. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- Recognizing when they need immediate help
- Contacting appropriate emergency services
- Not relying on MindMate AI for crisis intervention
- Seeking professional help when experiencing a mental health emergency

## 8. Limitations of Crisis Detection

Users should understand that:

- Crisis detection is automated and imperfect
- Not all crisis situations will be detected
- The AI cannot assess the severity of a situation
- Human judgment is essential in crisis situations

## 9. Data Handling in Crisis Situations

Conversations where crisis indicators are detected may be:

- Retained for safety and legal compliance purposes
- Reviewed to improve our crisis detection systems
- Subject to legal disclosure requirements

## 10. Updates to Crisis Resources

We strive to keep crisis resource information current but cannot guarantee the accuracy or availability of third-party services. Users should verify resources independently.
