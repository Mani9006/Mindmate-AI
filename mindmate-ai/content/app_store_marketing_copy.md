#### Our Story

```
## Our Story

MindMate AI was born from a simple, powerful observation: too many people struggle 
alone with their mental health.

In 2022, our founders—Dr. Sarah Chen, a clinical psychologist, and Alex Rivera, 
a machine learning engineer—came together with a shared vision. Sarah saw patients 
waiting weeks for appointments, struggling between sessions, and unable to afford 
ongoing care. Alex believed AI could bridge that gap.

They asked: What if everyone had access to compassionate mental health support, 
anytime they needed it? What if technology could make therapy's most powerful 
tools available to all?

MindMate AI launched in 2023 with that mission. Today, we've supported over 2 
million people across 50+ countries on their mental wellness journeys.

But we're just getting started.
```
