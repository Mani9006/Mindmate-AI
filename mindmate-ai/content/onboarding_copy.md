## PART 6: CONSENT AND PRIVACY EXPLANATION (Plain English)

### Consent and Privacy Screen

**Headline:** Your Privacy & Consent

**Subheadline:** Everything you need to know, explained in plain language

**Body Copy:**
Before we begin, we want to make sure you understand how MindMate AI works and how we handle your information. This is important, so please read carefully.

---

### What MindMate AI Is

**Section Title:** What MindMate AI Is

**Content:**
MindMate AI is an AI-powered mental wellness companion designed to provide support, guidance, and evidence-based techniques for improving your emotional wellbeing. It's like having a supportive friend, coach, or guide available whenever you need to talk.

**Important:** MindMate AI is NOT a replacement for professional mental health care. We're not doctors, therapists, or crisis counselors. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, we'll always direct you to appropriate professional resources.

---

### What We Collect and Why

**Section Title:** What We Collect and Why

**Content:**

**Your Conversations:**
We save your chat history so you can look back on your progress and so your MindMate companion can provide more personalized support over time. Your conversations help us understand your needs and tailor our responses.

**Assessment Responses:**
The answers you provide in questionnaires help us personalize your experience and suggest relevant techniques and topics.

**Usage Information:**
We collect basic information about how you use the app—like which features you use and how often—to help us improve MindMate AI for everyone.

**Account Information:**
If you create an account, we store your email and preferences so you can access your profile across devices.

---

### How We Protect Your Information

**Section Title:** How We Protect Your Information

**Content:**

**Encryption:**
Your data is encrypted both when it's being sent to us and when it's stored. This means it's scrambled into code that can't be easily read by anyone who shouldn't see it.

**No Selling Your Data:**
We will NEVER sell your personal information to advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties. Your conversations are yours.

**Limited Access:**
Only a small number of authorized team members can access user data, and only for specific purposes like troubleshooting or improving our service.

**Anonymized Research:**
Sometimes we use anonymized data (with all personal details removed) to improve our AI and understand how people use MindMate AI. This data cannot be traced back to you.

---

### When We Might Need to Share Information

**Section Title:** When We Might Need to Share Information

**Content:**

**If You're in Danger:**
If you indicate that you might harm yourself or others, we may need to share information with emergency services to help keep you safe. This is rare, but your safety is our priority.

**Legal Requirements:**
If we're required by law to share information (like in response to a court order), we may need to comply. This is very uncommon.

**With Your Permission:**
If you explicitly ask us to share information with someone (like your therapist), we can do that with your clear consent.

---

### Your Rights and Choices

**Section Title:** Your Rights and Choices

**Content:**

**Access Your Data:**
You can request a copy of all the data we have about you at any time.

**Delete Your Data:**
You can delete your account and all associated data whenever you want. We'll remove your information from our systems (though some anonymized data may remain for research purposes).

**Change Your Mind:**
You can update your privacy settings or withdraw consent for certain data uses at any time in your account settings.

**Take a Break:**
You can stop using MindMate AI whenever you want. There's no commitment.

---

### Understanding the Limits

**Section Title:** Understanding the Limits
