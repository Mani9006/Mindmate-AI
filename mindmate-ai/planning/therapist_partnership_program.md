# MindMate AI Licensed Therapist Oversight Program

## Executive Summary

The MindMate AI Licensed Therapist Oversight Program establishes a comprehensive framework for integrating licensed mental health professionals into our AI-powered therapy platform. This program ensures clinical excellence, regulatory compliance, and continuous improvement of our AI systems through expert human oversight.

---

## 1. Therapist Recruitment Strategy

### 1.1 Target Candidate Profiles

#### Tier 1: Clinical Advisors (Strategic Level)
| Attribute | Requirements |
|-----------|--------------|
| **License** | Active state licensure (LCSW, LMFT, LPCC, PsyD, PhD, MD) |
| **Experience** | 10+ years clinical practice |
| **Specialization** | Multiple modalities (CBT, DBT, ACT, etc.) |
| **Technology Comfort** | Experience with telehealth platforms |
| **Availability** | 5-10 hours/month for advisory duties |

#### Tier 2: Session Reviewers (Operational Level)
| Attribute | Requirements |
|-----------|--------------|
| **License** | Active state licensure in good standing |
| **Experience** | 3+ years post-licensure practice |
| **Documentation** | Strong clinical documentation skills |
| **Availability** | 10-20 hours/week for review work |
| **Specializations** | Crisis intervention, specific demographics |

#### Tier 3: Crisis Response Supervisors (Emergency Level)
| Attribute | Requirements |
|-----------|--------------|
| **License** | LCSW, LMFT, LPCC, or clinical psychologist |
| **Experience** | 5+ years with crisis intervention |
| **Certifications** | Suicide assessment certification preferred |
| **Availability** | On-call rotations, 24/7 coverage capability |
| **Response Time** | <15 minutes for crisis escalation |

### 3.2 Crisis Event Review Protocol

#### Immediate Response Workflow (0-15 minutes)

| Time | Action | Responsible Party |
|------|--------|-------------------|

| 3-5 min | Crisis supervisor reviews flagged content | Licensed therapist |
| 5-10 min | Risk assessment completed | Licensed therapist |
| 10-15 min | Intervention decision made | Licensed therapist |
