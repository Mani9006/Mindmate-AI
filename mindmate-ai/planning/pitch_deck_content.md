## Slide 1: The Problem - A Global Mental Health Crisis

### Headline
**The Mental Health Crisis Is the Defining Health Challenge of Our Generation**

### Key Statistics & Market Pain Points

#### The Scale of the Crisis
- **1 billion people** worldwide suffer from a mental health disorder (WHO)
- **1 in 5 adults** in the U.S. experience mental illness each year (NAMI)
- **Depression is the leading cause of disability** globally, affecting 280 million people
- **Suicide is the 4th leading cause of death** among 15-29 year olds
- **$1 trillion** lost annually in global productivity due to mental health conditions

#### The Access Gap
- **60% of U.S. counties** have no practicing psychiatrists
- **Average wait time** for therapy: 3-6 weeks; in rural areas: 3+ months
- **Cost barrier:** Average therapy session: $150-300; many insurance plans have limited coverage
- **Stigma prevents 67%** of people with mental health conditions from seeking help
- **Therapist shortage:** Only 30% of mental health needs are met globally

#### The Young Adult Crisis
- **Gen Z reports the worst mental health** of any generation (APA)
- **75% of mental health conditions** develop by age 24
- **College counseling centers** report 5x increase in demand over 10 years
- **Social media correlation:** 59% of teens report cyberbullying impacts mental health

### Problem Statement
> "We have a mental health epidemic with a massive supply-demand mismatch. Millions need help, but traditional care is too expensive, too slow, and too scarce."
